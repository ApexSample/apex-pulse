## About Apex Electronics & Apex Pulse

Apex Electronics is a fast-growing consumer electronics company pushing the boundaries of connected devices and digital experiences. Apex Pulse is our flagship product — a privacy-first fitness band with on-device AI that surfaces heart rate, SpO2, HRV, sleep stages, and activity data. No cloud. No data brokers. Just your health data, on your device.

We're building the next generation of the web experience for Apex Pulse, and the frontend is at the heart of making it exceptional.

---

## Role Overview

We're looking for a **Senior Frontend Engineer** to join the Apex Pulse team and become the go-to owner of our React/Next.js frontend architecture. You'll build performant, accessible, and privacy-first UIs that handle real-time health data — from data-dense dashboards to silky-smooth micro-interactions.

This is a high-impact IC role with a clear growth path toward **Frontend Tech Lead** as the team scales post-launch.

---

## What You'll Do

- **Own the frontend architecture** for Apex Pulse — establish patterns, standards, and best practices
- Build and ship high-quality, performant UI components using **React 18+** and **Next.js 14+** (App Router)
- Write **TypeScript in strict mode** by default, every day
- Build real-time data visualizations (heart rate, SpO2, HRV, sleep stages) that perform smoothly on mid-range devices
- Drive **Core Web Vitals** targets: Lighthouse ≥90, LCP <2.5s, CLS <0.1, bundle <200KB gzipped
- Implement a **privacy-first UI layer** — no cloud data leakage, graceful offline states, clean BLE/device-sync integration
- Ensure all UIs meet **WCAG 2.1 AA** accessibility standards
- Write and maintain frontend tests targeting ≥60% coverage (Jest + React Testing Library)
- Conduct thorough code reviews and raise the technical bar
- Mentor junior/mid engineers on frontend best practices
- Grow into a **Frontend Tech Lead** role within 12–18 months as the team expands

---

## Required Qualifications

- **5+ years** of professional frontend engineering experience
- Deep expertise in **React 18+** (hooks, context, concurrent features) and **Next.js 14+** (App Router, SSR, SSG, ISR)
- **TypeScript strict mode** — you write types by default, not as an afterthought
- **Tailwind CSS** proficiency
- Proven track record of **frontend performance optimization** — LCP, CLS, FID/INP, bundle analysis, lazy loading, caching
- Experience with **real-time or near-real-time data UIs** (WebSockets, SSE, polling strategies)
- **TanStack Query** (or similar) for server state management
- Frontend **testing** with Jest + React Testing Library
- **WCAG 2.1 AA** accessibility — not a checkbox, a craft
- Familiarity with **GitHub Actions** or similar CI/CD pipelines
- **Privacy-first engineering mindset** — you think about data minimization, third-party SDK risk, and user trust as first-class concerns
