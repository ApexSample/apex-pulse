🏢 Our Story

Built by people who believe privacy is a right

Apex Electronics was founded on one conviction: your health data belongs to you — and only you. Meet the team making that real.

"We started Apex because we were tired of trading our most personal data — our heartbeat, our sleep, our stress — for the privilege of wearing a device on our wrist."

The wearables industry built its business model on your data. Every step, every heartbeat, every night of poor sleep — uploaded, analyzed, monetized. We asked a simple question: what if it didn't have to be that way?


Apex Pulse is our answer. 100% on-device AI. Zero cloud sync for health data. A Cure53-audited privacy architecture. And a price that doesn't require a subscription to unlock what you've already paid for.
